About Me

My name is Kevin Wesley Miller and I am currently working to become a web developer. I have spent most of my career life working in the service industry as a repair technician. The game itself is almost 20 years old and was developed as freeware over the years and as a new member of the community I would like to take part and contribute what I can to a game that I truly enjoy.
